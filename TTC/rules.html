Rules & Policies



  • Rule 1.
    All TTC clients should be well aware the accompanying hunting party and/or machine operator are all highly trained skilled combatants and will not tolerate negligence.



  • Rule 2.
    Additional costs are liable to be integrated into the total trip price. Listen to the accompanying guide's instructions.



  • Rule 3.
    Don't talk about TTC. The TTC does not exist. Violators of this rule are subject to the TTC.



  • Rule 4.
    TTC operators are given the order to neutralize all potential threats. This includes but is not limited to; non-human beings, environmental dangers and the client.



  • Rule 5.
    Once the client leaves the present, he or she is no longer subject to existing laws. The TTC and her operatives share this same trait. Therefore, we are the only law. We are the judge, jury and executioner.
