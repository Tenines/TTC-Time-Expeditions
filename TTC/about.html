      About us

Officially founded in the underground tunnels of Boston in the year 1876, we have sacrificed generations of our lives to create the perfect fiction: a machine capable of breaking all establishments of material reality allowing us to transcend the binds of time.

We now use this technology for no ulterior motive and merely wish to give the gift of knowledge to the world.

       Thomas V. Williamson Hendricks IV

Janitor turned salesman, Tom is first and foremost a family man, giving it all so his two, six year old daughters can have a comfortable life. Being a single father has hardened Tom's will and with iron resolve he will protect clients with utmost service to keep his weak, sick, feeble, clinically ill daughters happy.

Thomas is not available on weekends as he bakes cakes with his daughters to fund their medical expenses.

         Jerry

Jerry isn't his real name. A veteran of 60 years trained by monks deep in the Tibetan mountains, Jerry soon found himself working as a mercenary for various criminal rings and private clients. With a reasonable headcount of 2368 over his career, Jerry is determined to keep his clients safe.

      Pyotr Solomon Preobrazhensky

Born somewhere in the Siberian Tundra at an unknown date, Pyotr is credited with and constantly reaffirms that he lifted 40 "belanskies" once. He has also never needed to bring a firearm into any hunt, crediting his overwhelming physical ability as the reason for his flawless success rate.

      Yggbeliath Ur Shin-Melial

      Shin-Melial is an otherworldly entity who arrived in an unmanned company time machine one day looking for a job. He remains adamant that his claim to his current occupation as an operative is centered around his casting out of "Helios Ten Abarath and Immortal Sun-Singer High Savant Uldrygg Ur Sin Shemenoth son of Uldrygg Ur-Rex Sin Baalaeghstus".

Our editor would like to note he does not know what this means, if anything.

Amongst "Shin-Melial's" expansive list of attributes clients should be most aware that according to our many, many tests; Melial is completely unkillable making him one of our strongest operators. The general consensus across the relatively decent span of clients who have traveled with Melial describe him as a "demonic hellspawn" and an "average guide".
Take that as you will.
